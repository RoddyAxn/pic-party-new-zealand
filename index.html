Why this matters

A nationwide platform for independent participation

Many voters want representatives who are answerable first to their electorate and community. This network is designed to help credible independent candidates connect, organise, share resources and present a clear alternative to politics as usual.

1

Local accountability

Candidates should listen locally, publish their positions clearly and remain directly answerable to voters.

2

National cooperation

Independent candidates can retain their local identity while cooperating on democratic standards and shared national concerns.

3

Affordable participation

A modest membership fee makes it easier for ordinary New Zealanders to take part and help build the organisation.
